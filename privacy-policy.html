Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

Gridiron Fitness ("Gridiron Fitness," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at gridironfitnesstx.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the Site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

The Site does not currently ask you to submit a form, create an account, or provide personal information directly. We do not run analytics or advertising tracking on this Site.

That said, some information is collected automatically or by third parties whose content appears on the Site:

  • Hosting logs. Our hosting provider (Netlify) may automatically log standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, for security and performance purposes.
  • Embedded Instagram content. The Site displays embedded posts from our Instagram account. Instagram (Meta Platforms, Inc.) may set cookies or collect data when that embedded content loads, governed by Instagram's own Privacy Policy, not this one.
  • Outbound links. Buttons on the Site link out to Google Maps, Facebook, and Instagram. Once you click through, your activity on those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

How We Use Information

Any technical information collected through hosting logs is used solely to operate, secure, and maintain the Site. We do not sell, rent, or share visitor information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

The Site itself does not set its own tracking or advertising cookies. The embedded Instagram widget may set cookies controlled by Instagram. You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
